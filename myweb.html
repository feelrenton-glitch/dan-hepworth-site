Kubrick's GOAT Case

  1. He mastered multiple genres.
    • Sci-fi with 2001: A Space Odyssey
    • Psychological horror with The Shining
    • War films with Paths of Glory and Full Metal Jacket
  2. His movies look instantly recognizable.
    • Symmetry and one-point perspective
    • Iconic set design and visual control
    • A mood that feels cold, intense, and unforgettable
  3. He made films that stay in culture for decades.
    • The Shining still shapes horror imagery
    • 2001 still feels futuristic
    • Directors across generations still borrow from his style
